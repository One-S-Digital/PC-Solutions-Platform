# Canton-Level Qualification Equivalences (Switzerland)

Swiss childcare qualifications vary by canton. The following table maps common equivalences.

## Federal Diplomas (recognised nationwide)
- EDE ES — Éducateur·trice de l'enfance (École supérieure)
- EDE HES — Bachelor en travail social, orientation petite enfance
- ASSC CFC — Certificat fédéral de capacité

## Canton-Specific Recognitions

### Geneva (GE)
- EDE ES/HES required for head educator positions.
- ASSC minimum for autonomous group supervision.
- Ratio: 1 EDE per 6 children (0–18 months), 1 per 8 (18–36 months).

### Vaud (VD)
- ASSC or EDE required for all counted staff.
- Auxiliaires capped at 20% of team.
- Ratio governed by RSPE (Règlement sur l'accueil de jour des enfants).

### Fribourg (FR)
- Bilingual canton — FR/DE qualifications both accepted.
- EDE or equivalent required for director role.

### Neuchâtel (NE)
- EDE or ASSC for counted staff.
- Ratio: 1 adult per 5 children under 18 months, 1 per 7 above.

### Valais (VS)
- Canton accepts ASSC and EDE; auxiliary maximum 33% of team.

### Bern (BE)
- FaBe (Fachfrau/-mann Betreuung) — German-language equivalent of ASSC.
- Kindererzieher HF — equivalent of EDE ES.
