# Swiss Childcare Role Taxonomy

## EDE (Éducateur·trice de l'enfance diplômé·e)
Fully qualified childcare educator with federal diploma (ES/HES level).
Responsible for pedagogical leadership, child development observation, and parent communication.

## ASSC (Assistant·e en soins et santé communautaire)
Community health and care assistant. Works under EDE supervision.
Handles daily care routines, feeding, hygiene, and basic developmental support.

## Stagiaire / Apprenti·e
Intern or apprentice in vocational training (formation duale or full-time school).
Supervised at all times. Cannot be counted toward minimum staffing ratios.

## Auxiliaire
Untrained auxiliary staff. Limited autonomous responsibility.
Supplements qualified staff but does not replace them in ratio calculations.

## Directeur·trice / Responsable de structure
Site director. Responsible for compliance, team management, and liaison with the foundation.

## Éducateur·trice remplaçant·e
Replacement educator covering absences. Must hold at minimum ASSC qualification for ratio compliance.
