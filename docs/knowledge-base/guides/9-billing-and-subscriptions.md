## Managing Your Subscription

### Viewing Your Current Plan

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Click **Billing & Subscription**
3. See your subscription details:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Current Subscription                               │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                     │
│  Plan: 🌿 Essential                                │
│  Status: Active ✅                                  │
│                                                     │
│  Current Period: Jan 1 - Jan 31, 2025              │
│  Cancel at Period End: No                          │
│                                                     │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Subscription Information

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Plan** | Your current plan name |
| **Status** | Active, Inactive, Trial, etc. |
| **Current Period** | Billing period dates |
| **Cancel at Period End** | Whether cancellation is scheduled |

### Subscription Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| ✅ **Active** | Subscription is working |
| ⏳ **Pending** | Awaiting activation |
| 🆓 **Trial** | In trial period |
| ⚠️ **Past Due** | Payment is overdue |
| ⏸️ **Paused** | Temporarily paused |
| ❌ **Cancelled** | Subscription ended |
| ⌛ **Grace Period** | Post-payment grace period |

---

### Upgrading Your Plan

To access more features, upgrade your plan:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing & Subscription**
2. View available plans
3. Click **"Upgrade"** on the plan you want
4. Review the changes
5. Confirm the upgrade

> 💡 **Tip:** Your new features are available immediately after upgrade confirmation.

### Downgrading Your Plan

To move to a lower-tier plan:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing & Subscription**
2. Click **"Downgrade"** on the desired plan
3. Review what features you'll lose
4. Confirm the downgrade

> ⚠️ **Note:** Downgrades typically take effect at the end of your current billing period.

## Annual vs. Monthly Billing

### Billing Cycle Options

| Cycle | Description | Savings |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Monthly** | Billed every month | Standard price |
| **Annual** | Billed once per year | ~10% savings |

### Switching Billing Cycles

To change from monthly to annual (or vice versa):

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing & Subscription**
2. Click **"Change Billing Cycle"**
3. Select your preference
4. Confirm the change

> 💡 **Tip:** Annual billing offers approximately 10% savings compared to monthly.

### Pro-rated Billing

When changing plans mid-cycle:
- Upgrades: You're charged the difference
- Downgrades: Credit applied to future billing

## Subscription Requests

### How Subscription Activation Works

1. **Request** - You request a subscription
2. **Review** - Admin reviews your request
3. **Invoice** - Invoice is sent
4. **Payment** - You pay the invoice
5. **Activation** - Subscription is activated

### Requesting a Subscription

If you don't have a subscription yet:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing & Subscription**
2. Click **"Request Subscription"**
3. Select your desired plan
4. Choose billing period (Monthly/Quarterly/Annual)
5. Provide contact information
6. Add a message (optional)
7. Submit request

### Request Status

Track your request status:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| ⏳ **Pending** | Submitted, awaiting review |
| 🔍 **Under Review** | Admin is reviewing |
| 📧 **Invoice Sent** | Invoice sent to you |
| 💳 **Payment Pending** | Awaiting your payment |
| ✅ **Payment Received** | Payment confirmed |
| 🎉 **Activated** | Subscription is active! |
| ❌ **Declined** | Request was declined |

### Enterprise Inquiries

For Enterprise plans:
1. Click **"Contact Us"** on the Enterprise plan
2. Fill in the inquiry form
3. Our team will contact you
4. Custom terms are arranged

## Cancellation

### Requesting Cancellation

If you need to cancel your subscription:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing & Subscription**
2. Click **"Cancel Subscription"**
3. Provide a reason (optional)
4. Confirm cancellation request

### What Happens After Cancellation

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Confirm Cancellation                               │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                     │
│  ⚠️ Are you sure you want to cancel?               │
│                                                     │
│  Your subscription will remain active until:        │
│  January 31, 2025                                   │
│                                                     │
│  After this date, you will lose access to:          │
│  • Parent Leads                                     │
│  • HR Documents                                     │
│  • Recruitment Module                               │
│  • E-Learning                                       │
│  • Analytics                                        │
│                                                     │
│  You'll still have access to:                       │
│  • Settings                                         │
│  • Profile Management                               │
│  • Support                                          │
│                                                     │
│  Reason for cancellation (optional):               │
│  ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐   │
│  │                                             │   │
│  └─────────────────────────────────────────────┘   │
│                                                     │
│     [Keep Subscription]    [Yes, Cancel]           │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Cancellation Timeline

1. **Request submitted** - Your cancellation is logged
2. **Review** - Admin processes the request
3. **Confirmation** - You receive confirmation
4. **End of period** - Access continues until billing period ends
5. **Cancelled** - Subscription is cancelled

### Reactivating After Cancellation

Changed your mind?

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing & Subscription**
2. Click **"Reactivate Subscription"**
3. Select your plan
4. Complete the process
