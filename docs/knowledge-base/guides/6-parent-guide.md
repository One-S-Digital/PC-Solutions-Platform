## Finding a Daycare

ProCrèche Solutions makes it easy to find the right daycare for your family.

### How the Matching System Works

1. You submit an enquiry with your requirements
2. Your enquiry is matched to suitable daycares
3. Interested daycares respond to you
4. You communicate and arrange visits

### Lead Submission Form

To find a daycare, submit an enquiry with your needs.

#### Accessing the Form

1. Click **"Find a Daycare"** on your dashboard, OR
2. Navigate to the enquiry submission page

#### Parent Information

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Your Name** | Your full name | ✅ Yes |
| **Email** | Contact email | ✅ Yes |
| **Phone** | Contact number | ❌ Optional |

#### Child Information

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Child's Name** | Your child's first name | ✅ Yes |
| **Child's Age** | Age in months or years | ✅ Yes |

#### Preferences

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Preferred Location** | Canton or region | ✅ Yes |
| **Desired Start Date** | When you need daycare | ✅ Yes |
| **Preferred Languages** | Languages for your child | ❌ Optional |

#### Special Requirements

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Special Requirements** | Allergies, medical needs, dietary restrictions, etc. |
| **Message to Daycares** | Personal note to introduce your family |

### Submitting Your Enquiry

1. Fill in all required fields
2. Add any special requirements
3. Write a personal message (recommended)
4. Click **"Submit Enquiry"**
5. Receive confirmation

### What Happens Next

After submitting:
1. ✉️ You receive a confirmation email
2. 🔍 Your enquiry is matched to suitable daycares
3. 📋 Matched daycares receive your enquiry
4. 💬 Interested daycares respond to you
5. 📱 You're notified of responses

## My Enquiries

Track all your daycare enquiries in one place.

### Accessing Enquiries

Click **My Enquiries** in the sidebar.

### Enquiry List

View all your submitted enquiries with:
- Child's name
- Preferred location
- Submission date
- Number of responses
- Status

### Enquiry Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 📤 **Sent** | Submitted and sent to daycares |
| 👀 **Viewed** | Daycares have seen it |
| 💬 **Responses Received** | You have replies |
| ✅ **Enrolled** | Successfully enrolled |
| ❌ **Closed** | No longer active |

### Viewing Responses

1. Click on an enquiry
2. See all daycare responses
3. Each response includes:
   - Daycare name
   - Response message
   - Contact information
   - Response status

### Response Types

Daycares may respond with:

| Response | What it means |
|----------|---------------|
| **Interested** | They'd like to connect with you |
| **Not Interested** | Not a fit (full capacity, etc.) |
| **Needs More Info** | They have questions |
| **Enrolled** | Enrollment confirmed! |

### Communicating with Daycares

After receiving a response:
1. Read the daycare's message
2. Click **"Reply"** or **"Message"**
3. Continue the conversation
4. Arrange a visit if interested

### Marking Favorites

Found a daycare you like?
1. Click the heart/star icon
2. Access favorites from your dashboard

## Support & Help

### Creating a Support Ticket

Having trouble? We're here to help!

1. Click **Support** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Create Ticket"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Subject** - Brief description
   - **Category** - General, Technical, etc.
   - **Message** - Detailed explanation
4. Submit

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many daycares will see my enquiry?**
A: Enquiries are matched to daycares based on your location and requirements. Multiple suitable daycares may receive your enquiry.

**Q: How long until I hear from daycares?**
A: Response times vary. Most daycares respond within 1-2 weeks. Check your enquiries regularly for updates.

**Q: Can I edit my enquiry after submitting?**
A: You can submit a new enquiry with updated information. Contact support if you need to modify an existing enquiry.

**Q: Is there a cost to use the platform?**
A: No! Parent access is completely free.

**Q: What if no daycares respond?**
A: Try expanding your search area or adjusting your requirements. You can also submit a new enquiry.

## Tips for Finding the Right Daycare

### Before You Start

1. **Know your needs** - Full-time, part-time, specific days?
2. **Set your budget** - Understand typical costs in your area
3. **List priorities** - Location, pedagogy, languages, etc.
4. **Consider timing** - Start your search early (3-6 months ahead)

### Writing a Good Enquiry

1. **Be specific** about your needs
2. **Mention special requirements** upfront
3. **Share about your family** - help daycares know you
4. **Be flexible** if possible on start dates

### After Receiving Responses

1. **Respond to all** - Even if not interested (be polite)
2. **Schedule visits** - See the daycare in person
3. **Ask questions** - Staff ratios, daily routines, etc.
4. **Trust your instincts** - Choose where your child will thrive

### Questions to Ask Daycares

- What is your daily routine?
- What is your staff-to-child ratio?
- What pedagogical approach do you follow?
- How do you handle nap times and meals?
- What are your policies on illness?
- How do you communicate with parents?
- What are the fees and what's included?
