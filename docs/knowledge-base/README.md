## 📚 Choose Your Role

Select your role to find guides tailored to you:

### 🏫 [Foundation (Daycare)](./en/guides/2-foundation-daycare-guide.md)
For daycare centers and childcare organizations.
- Manage parent leads and enquiries
- Post job listings and recruit educators
- Browse marketplace for products and services
- Access e-learning and HR resources

### 📦 [Product Supplier](./en/guides/3-product-supplier-guide.md)
For companies selling products to daycares.
- List and manage your products
- Handle orders and inquiries
- Create promo codes
- Track analytics and performance

### 🔧 [Service Provider](./en/guides/4-service-provider-guide.md)
For companies offering services to daycares.
- Manage your service listings
- Handle service requests
- Set up booking integrations
- Create promotional offers

### 👩‍🏫 [Educator (Candidate)](./en/guides/5-educator-candidate-guide.md)
For childcare professionals seeking employment.
- Browse and apply to job listings
- Manage your professional profile
- Set your availability schedule
- Track your applications

### 👨‍👩‍👧 [Parent](./en/guides/6-parent-guide.md)
For parents looking for daycare services.
- Submit enquiries to find daycares
- Track responses from centers
- Communicate with daycares
