## Organization Profile

Your company profile showcases your services to potential customers.

### Accessing Your Profile

1. Click **Organization Profile** in the sidebar, OR
2. Go to **Settings** → **Edit Profile**

### Quick shortcut to manage services (new)

From the **Edit Profile** page, you can now jump directly to your service management area:

1. Open your **Organization Profile**
2. Click **Edit Profile**
3. Click **My Services** (next to **Save Changes**)
4. You will be taken to **Service Listings**, where you can add and manage services

### Profile Information

#### Basic Company Details

| Field | Description | Tips |
|-------|-------------|------|

| **Phone Number** | Business phone | Include country code |
| **Contact Email** | Public email | For service inquiries |
| **Canton/Region** | Your location | Where you're based |

## Service Listings

Manage your services that appear in the marketplace.

### Accessing Services

You can access Service Listings in either of these ways:

1. Click **Service Listings** in the sidebar, or
2. Go to **Settings** → **Edit Profile** → **My Services**

### Creating Services

1. Click **"Create Service"** / **"Add New Service"**
2. Fill in service details:

#### Basic Information

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Title** | Service name (max 100 characters) | ✅ Yes |
| **Description** | Full service description (max 1000 characters) | ✅ Yes |
| **Service Categories** | One or more categories (you can add custom) | ✅ Yes |
| **Delivery Type** | On-site, Remote, or Hybrid | ✅ Recommended |
| **Availability** | When you can deliver the service | ✅ Yes |
| **Pricing details** | e.g., CHF 150/hour or Contact for quote | ❌ Optional |
| **Tags** | Comma-separated keywords | ❌ Optional |
| **Service image** | Image shown on the marketplace card | ❌ Optional |

#### Full “Add New Service” form walkthrough (recommended)

When the **Add New Service** modal opens, complete it like this:

1. **Service title** (required): short and clear, max 100 characters (e.g., “Deep Cleaning for Daycares”)
2. **Description** (required): what’s included, what’s not included, and typical duration (max 1000 characters)
3. **Service categories** (required): pick from suggestions or type your own and press Enter
4. **Delivery type**: choose On-site / Remote / Hybrid
5. **Availability & schedule** (required): e.g., “Weekdays 9:00–18:00” or “By appointment”
6. **Pricing details** (optional): e.g., “CHF 150/hour” or “Contact for quote”
7. **Tags** (optional): e.g., “cleaning, eco, bilingual”
8. **Service image** (optional): upload a JPG/PNG so your listing looks professional
9. Click **Add** (or **Save Changes** when editing)

#### Service Image

- Upload a professional image representing your service
- Recommended: 800x600px minimum

3. Set service status:

| Status | Visibility |
|--------|------------|
| **Active** | Visible in marketplace |
| **Inactive** | Hidden from marketplace |
| **Draft** | Not published yet |

4. Click **"Save Service"**

### Managing Services

#### Editing a Service
1. Find the service in your list
2. Click **"Edit"**
3. Make changes
4. Save

#### Changing Visibility
1. Click the status badge
2. Select Active or Inactive

#### Deleting a Service
1. Click **"Delete"**
2. Confirm deletion

### Delivery Types Explained

| Type | Description | Best For |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **On-site** | You travel to the daycare | Training, Cleaning, Repairs |
| **Remote** | Delivered virtually | Consulting, Online Training |
| **Hybrid** | Mix of both | Flexible services |

## Service Request Management

Handle service requests from foundations.

### Accessing Requests

Click **Service Requests** in the sidebar.

### Understanding Requests

When a foundation wants your service, they submit a request with:
- Requested service
- Preferred date/time
- Specific requirements
- Additional notes

### Request Statuses

| Status | Meaning | Your Action |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| 🆕 **New** | Just received | Review and respond |
| ⏳ **Pending** | Awaiting your response | Accept or decline |
| ✅ **Accepted** | You confirmed | Schedule the service |
| 📅 **Scheduled** | Date confirmed | Deliver the service |
| ✅ **Completed** | Service delivered | Mark complete |
| ❌ **Declined** | You declined | No action needed |
| 🚫 **Cancelled** | Customer cancelled | No action needed |

### Handling a Request

#### To Accept a Request:
1. Open the request
2. Review the details
3. Click **"Accept"**
4. Propose date/time if needed
5. Confirm

#### To Decline a Request:
1. Open the request
2. Click **"Decline"**
3. Provide a reason (optional)
4. Confirm

### Scheduling Appointments

After accepting:
1. Coordinate with the foundation
2. Confirm the date and time
3. Update the request status to **"Scheduled"**
4. Add appointment details

### Completing a Service

After delivery:
1. Open the request
2. Click **"Mark as Completed"**
3. Add completion notes (optional)
4. Save

## Booking Link Integration

Connect your scheduling system for easy appointment booking.

### Supported Platforms

- **Calendly** - Popular scheduling tool
- **Cal.com** - Open-source alternative
- **Custom Links** - Your own booking system

### Setting Up Your Booking Link

1. Go to **Organization Profile** or **Settings**
2. Find **Booking Link** field
3. Paste your scheduling URL
4. Save

### How It Works

1. Foundations see a **"Book Now"** button on your profile
2. Clicking opens your scheduling page
3. They select a time that works
4. You receive a booking notification

> 💡 **Tip:** Make sure your availability is up to date in your scheduling system.

## Promo Codes

Create promotional codes to attract customers. Promo codes appear on your public profile.

### Accessing Promo Codes

Go to **Settings** → **Promo Code Manager**

### Creating a Promo Code

1. Click **"Add New Code"**
2. Fill in the modal:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│         Add New Promo Code                      [X] │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                     │
│  Code *                                             │
│  ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐   │
│  │  FIRST50                                     │   │
│  └─────────────────────────────────────────────┘   │
│  The code customers will use (e.g., FIRST50)       │
│                                                     │
│  Discount *                                         │
│  ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐   │
│  │  50% off your first consultation            │   │
│  └─────────────────────────────────────────────┘   │
│  Describe the discount customers will receive      │
│                                                     │
│  Description (Optional)                             │
│  ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐   │
│  │  Valid for new customers. One-time use      │   │
│  │  only. Must mention code when booking.      │   │
│  └─────────────────────────────────────────────┘   │
│  Optional details about terms or conditions        │
│                                                     │
│  ☑ Show on profile                                 │
│                                                     │
│         [Cancel]              [Save]               │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

#### Promo Code Fields

| Field | Description | Required | Example |
|-------|-------------|----------|---------|
| **Code** | The code customers use | ✅ Yes | `FIRST50`, `FREEEVAL`, `NEWYEAR` |
| **Discount** | What the customer gets | ✅ Yes | "50% off", "Free 30-min consultation" |
| **Description** | Terms and conditions | ❌ Optional | "New customers only" |
| **Show on Profile** | Display publicly | ✅ Yes (default: on) | Toggle on/off |

3. Click **"Save"**

### Managing Promo Codes

Your codes appear in a table:

| Code | Discount | Status | Actions |
|------|----------|--------|---------|
| `FIRST50` | 50% off first consultation | 🟢 Active | Edit · Delete |
| `FREEEVAL` | Free initial evaluation | 🟢 Active | Edit · Delete |

**Actions:**
- **Edit** - Modify code details
- **Delete** - Remove the code
- **Hide** - Uncheck "Show on profile" to hide without deleting

### Promo Code Ideas for Service Providers

| Code | Discount Description |
|------|---------------------|
| `FIRST50` | 50% off your first session |
| `FREEEVAL` | Free initial consultation |
| `NEWCLIENT` | CHF 50 off first booking |
| `BUNDLE3` | Book 3 sessions, get 10% off |
| `REFERRAL` | 20% off when referred |

## Discount Terminations

### What Are Discount Terminations?

Discount terminations are formal endings of promotional pricing or special discount arrangements.

### When They Occur

- Promotional periods expire
- Special pricing agreements end
- Partnership terms change
- Volume discount thresholds not met

### How It Works

1. Administered through the platform
2. You receive advance notification
3. Standard pricing resumes
4. Records are maintained for reference

### What This Means for You

- Check notifications for termination notices
- Update your promo codes accordingly
- Communicate changes to customers if needed
- Contact support with questions
