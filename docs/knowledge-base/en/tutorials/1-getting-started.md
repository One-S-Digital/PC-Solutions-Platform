## Platform Overview

### What is ProCrèche Solutions?

ProCrèche Solutions is an all-in-one platform connecting the Swiss childcare ecosystem:

- **Daycares (Foundations)** find products, services, staff, and manage parent enquiries
- **Product Suppliers** sell childcare products to daycares
- **Service Providers** offer professional services to daycares
- **Educators** find job opportunities at daycares
- **Parents** find quality daycare for their children

### Supported Languages

The platform is available in three languages:
- 🇬🇧 **English**
- 🇫🇷 **Français** (French)
- 🇩🇪 **Deutsch** (German)

You can change your language at any time using the language switcher in the navigation bar.

## Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Go to the Sign Up Page

1. Visit the ProCrèche Solutions website
2. Click the **"Sign Up"** button in the top navigation bar
3. You'll be taken to the registration page

### Step 2: Choose Your Role

Select the type of account that matches your needs:

| Role | Who is this for? |
|------|------------------|
| 🏫 **Foundation** | Daycare centers, childcare organizations |
| 📦 **Product Supplier** | Companies selling products to daycares |
| 🔧 **Service Provider** | Companies offering services to daycares |
| 👩‍🏫 **Educator** | Childcare professionals seeking jobs |
| 👨‍👩‍👧 **Parent** | Parents looking for daycare services |

Click on your role to proceed to the registration form.

### Step 3: Fill in Your Details

The information required depends on your role:

#### For Foundations, Suppliers & Service Providers:

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Organization Name | Your company or daycare name | ✅ Yes |
| Contact Person | Your full name | ✅ Yes |
| Email | Your email address (used for login) | ✅ Yes |
| Password | Create a secure password (min. 8 characters) | ✅ Yes |
| Confirm Password | Re-enter your password | ✅ Yes |
| Phone Number | Business contact number | ✅ Yes |
| Canton/Region | Your location in Switzerland | ✅ Yes |
| Capacity | Number of children (Foundations only) | ✅ Yes |
| Category | Product category (Suppliers only) | ✅ Yes |
| Service Type | Type of service (Providers only) | ✅ Yes |

#### For Educators:

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Full Name | Your first and last name | ✅ Yes |
| Email | Your email address | ✅ Yes |
| Password | Create a secure password | ✅ Yes |
| Confirm Password | Re-enter your password | ✅ Yes |

#### For Parents:

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Parent Name | Your full name | ✅ Yes |
| Email | Your email address | ✅ Yes |
| Password | Create a secure password | ✅ Yes |
| Confirm Password | Re-enter your password | ✅ Yes |
| Child's Age | Age of your child | ✅ Yes |
| Preferred Start Date | When you need daycare | ✅ Yes |

### Step 4: Accept Terms & Complete CAPTCHA

1. ☑️ Check the box to accept the **Terms of Service**
2. Complete the **CAPTCHA verification** to prove you're human
3. Click **"Create Account"**

### Step 5: Verify Your Email

After clicking "Create Account", you'll see the **Email Verification** screen.

#### What happens:

1. A **6-digit verification code** is sent to your email address
2. Check your email inbox (and spam/junk folder if needed)
3. The email will be from ProCrèche Solutions with subject: "Verify your email"

#### Email Verification Screen:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                                     │
│              ✉️ Verify Your Email                    │
│                                                     │
│  We've sent a verification code to:                 │
│  your.email@example.com                             │
│                                                     │
│  Please enter the code below:                       │
│                                                     │
│  ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐   │
│  │              [0] [0] [0] [0] [0] [0]        │   │
│  └─────────────────────────────────────────────┘   │
│                                                     │
│  ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐   │
│  │            [ Verify Email ]                  │   │
│  └─────────────────────────────────────────────┘   │
│                                                     │
│  Didn't receive the code?                          │
│  [Resend code] (available in 30s)                  │
│                                                     │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

#### To verify your email:

1. Open your email and find the verification email
2. Copy the **6-digit code** from the email
3. Enter the code in the verification field
4. Click **"Verify Email"**

#### Resending the Code:

If you didn't receive the email:

1. Wait 30 seconds (a countdown will show)
2. Click **"Resend code"**
3. Check your email again
4. Also check your **Spam/Junk** folder

#### Code Expired?

Verification codes expire after a few minutes. If your code has expired:

1. Click **"Resend code"** to get a new one
2. Use the new code to verify

> 💡 **Tip:** The code is typically valid for 10 minutes. Enter it as soon as you receive it.

### Step 6: Account Setup Complete!

After successful verification:

1. Your account is created
2. You'll see a **"Success!"** message
3. Click **"Go to Dashboard"** to access your account

> ⏳ **Please wait:** The system takes a few seconds to set up your account after verification. You'll see a progress indicator.

1. Go to the login page

4. Click **"Sign In"**

1. Click **"Continue with Google"**

Your session remains active as you navigate the platform. You'll be automatically signed out after a period of inactivity for security.

## Recovering a Forgotten Password

### Step 1: Request Password Reset

1. On the login page, click **"Forgot Password?"**
2. Enter your email address
3. Click **"Send Reset Link"**

### Step 2: Check Your Email

1. Look for an email from ProCrèche Solutions
2. Subject: "Reset your password"
3. Click the **reset link** in the email

### Step 3: Create New Password

1. Enter your **new password** (minimum 8 characters)
2. **Confirm** your new password
3. Click **"Reset Password"**

### Step 4: Sign In

1. You'll be redirected to the login page
2. Sign in with your new password

> 💡 **Tip:** Password reset links expire after a short time. Request a new one if yours has expired.

## Account Security

### Password Requirements

Your password must:
- Be at least **8 characters** long
- Not be a commonly used password
- Not have been exposed in a data breach

### Changing Your Password

1. Go to **Settings** → **Account Security**
2. Enter your **current password**
3. Enter your **new password**
4. Confirm your new password
5. Click **"Update Password"**

### Changing Your Email Address

1. Go to **Settings** → **Account Security**
2. Scroll to **"Change Email Address"**
3. Enter your new email address
4. Click **"Send Verification Code"**
5. Check your **new email** for a verification code
6. Enter the 6-digit code
7. Click **"Verify & Change Email"**

> ⚠️ **Important:** You must verify your new email address before the change takes effect.

### Best Practices

- ✅ Use a unique password for your account
- ✅ Don't share your password with others
- ✅ Sign out when using shared computers
- ✅ Keep your email address up to date

## Completing Your Profile

After signing up, we recommend completing your profile for the best experience.

### Why Complete Your Profile?

- ✅ **Foundations:** Appear in parent searches, attract candidates
- ✅ **Suppliers/Providers:** Get discovered by potential customers
- ✅ **Educators:** Stand out to employers
- ✅ **Parents:** Get better daycare matches

### How to Complete Your Profile

1. Click your **profile picture** (top right)
2. Select **"Edit Profile"**
3. Fill in all sections
4. Upload a profile photo
5. Click **"Save"**
