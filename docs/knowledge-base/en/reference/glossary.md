# Glossary

A guide to terms and definitions used on ProCrèche Solutions.

---

## A

### Active (Status)
A status indicating that an item is currently live and visible. For products, this means they're shown in the marketplace. For subscriptions, this means the plan is working.

### Admin
A platform administrator with elevated permissions to manage users, content, and system settings.

### Application
A formal submission by an educator to express interest in a job listing. Contains cover letter, CV, and other relevant information.

### Application Pipeline
The multi-stage hiring process for job applications: PENDING → REVIEWED → SHORTLISTED → INTERVIEW → OFFER → HIRED (or REJECTED at any stage). Replaces the old two-step ACCEPTED/REJECTED flow.

---

## B

### Basic Plan
The entry-level subscription plan for Foundations, including marketplace access and state policies.

### Billing Period
The time span for which you're charged (monthly, quarterly, or annually).

---

## C

### Canton
A Swiss administrative region or state. Switzerland has 26 cantons.

### Candidate
An educator who is seeking employment at daycares. Also referred to as "Educator" on the platform.

### Candidate Pool
A feature that allows Foundations to browse profiles of all available educators, not just those who have applied to their jobs. Candidates are ranked by role match, city overlap, skills, availability, and recency.

### CDI (Contrat à Durée Indéterminée)
A permanent employment contract with no fixed end date.

### CDD (Contrat à Durée Déterminée)
A fixed-term employment contract with a specified end date.

### Cover Image
A wide banner image that appears at the top of profile pages.

---

## D

### Dashboard
The main home screen after logging in, displaying key metrics, recent activity, and quick actions relevant to your role.

### Daycare
See "Foundation."

### Delivery Type
How a service is provided: On-site (at the customer's location), Remote (virtual), or Hybrid (combination).

### Digest
A summary email containing recent activity, sent daily or weekly based on your preferences.

### Draft
A saved item that hasn't been published yet. Draft jobs, products, or services are not visible to others.

---

## E

### E-Learning
An online education module offering courses, videos, and training materials for daycare professionals.

### Educator
A childcare professional seeking employment. Also called a "Candidate."

### Parent Inquiry
A request from a parent seeking daycare services. Also called a "Lead."

### Enterprise Plan
The highest subscription tier for Foundations, offering custom features and dedicated support.

### Essential Plan
The mid-tier subscription plan for Foundations, including parent leads and HR documents.

---

## F

### Foundation
A daycare organization or childcare center. The primary customer type on the platform.

### Fulfilled
A status indicating that an order has been delivered or a service has been completed.

---

## G

### GDPR
General Data Protection Regulation. A European privacy law that gives users control over their personal data.

### Grace Period
A short period after a subscription payment is due, during which access is maintained while payment is processed.

---

## H

### HR Procedures
Human Resources documents and templates available to subscribed Foundations.

---

## I

### Inactive (Status)
A status indicating that an item is not currently visible or available.

### Intern Pool
A pool of intern-level candidates available to Foundations for placement and visible to administrators via the Admin Intern Pool page (`/intern-pool`).

### Supplier Inquiry
A question or request for information sent to a supplier or service provider before placing an order.

---

## J

### Job Board
A listing of available job positions posted by Foundations.

### Job Listing
A published employment opportunity at a daycare.

---

## L

### Lead
A parent enquiry seeking daycare services. Foundations receive leads based on matching criteria.

### Lead Matching
The automatic process of connecting parent enquiries with suitable daycares based on location and requirements.

---

## M

### Marketplace
The section of the platform where Foundations can browse and purchase products and services.

### Message
A communication sent between users through the platform's messaging system.

---

## N

### Notification
A persistent alert stored in the `Notification` table about important activity, such as new messages, orders, or applications. Each notification has a type, title, body, optional link, and a `readAt` timestamp to track whether it has been seen. Notifications persist across sessions (unlike transient UI alerts).

---

## O

### Order
A purchase of products from a supplier.

### Organization
A business entity on the platform, such as a Foundation, Supplier, or Service Provider.

### Out of Stock
A product status indicating the item is temporarily unavailable.

---

## P

### Parent
A user seeking daycare services for their child.

### Pedagogy
The approach or method of teaching used by a daycare. Examples include Montessori, Reggio Emilia, and Waldorf.

### Pending
A status indicating that an item is awaiting action or processing.

### Premium
Features or content available only to subscribed users.

### Product
An item listed for sale by a Product Supplier.

### Product Supplier
A vendor that sells products (toys, furniture, supplies, etc.) to daycares.

### Professional Plan
The advanced subscription plan for Foundations, including recruitment, e-learning, and analytics.

### Profile
Your personal or organization information page that may be visible to other users.

### Promo Code
A promotional code offering a discount, created by suppliers or service providers.

### Published
A status indicating that an item is live and visible to other users.

---

## R

### Recruitment Module
A feature allowing Foundations to post jobs and manage applications from educators. Now called **Staffing** in the Foundation navigation.

### Region
A geographic area, often used to describe service areas (see also "Canton").

### Replacement Match
A scored candidate suggested for a Replacement Request. The match progresses through statuses: PROPOSED → OFFERED → ACCEPTED (or DECLINED). A Foundation sends an offer to a proposed candidate; the candidate accepts or declines; and the Foundation then confirms to finalise the placement.

### Replacement Request
An urgent staffing request posted by a Foundation when they need short-notice cover. Contains the role, date range, urgency level (LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH/CRITICAL), cities, and region. Status progresses: OPEN → MATCHED → CONFIRMED → FULFILLED.

### Request
A submission asking for a service from a Service Provider.

### Role
Your user type on the platform (Foundation, Supplier, Service Provider, Educator, Parent, Admin).

---

## S

### Service
An offering listed by a Service Provider (training, consulting, etc.).

### Staffing Module
The renamed and expanded recruitment section, now accessible via the **Staffing** collapsible group in the Foundation sidebar. Includes job posting (Post a Job), candidate browsing (Find Candidates), full application pipeline (Review Applications), replacement staffing (Replacements), and intern candidate browsing (Intern Pool).

### Service Provider
A vendor that offers services (training, consulting, cleaning, etc.) to daycares.

### Service Request
A Foundation's request for a service from a Service Provider.

### State Policies
Government regulations and guidelines specific to each Swiss canton regarding childcare.

### Status
The current condition or stage of an item (Active, Pending, Completed, etc.).

### Subscription
A paid plan that provides access to platform features.

### Support Ticket
A formal request for help from the support team.

---

## T

### Team Management
A feature allowing organization administrators to manage multiple users under one account.

### Terms of Service
The legal agreement that users accept when creating an account.

### Trial
A free period to test subscription features before committing.

---

## U

### User
Any person with an account on the platform.

---

## V

### VAT Number
Value Added Tax identification number used for invoicing.

### Verification
The process of confirming identity or information, such as email verification.

### Verification Code
A 6-digit code sent via email to verify your identity.

---

## W

### Webhook
A technical mechanism for automatic data synchronization (used behind the scenes).

---

## Status Definitions Quick Reference

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Active** | Live, visible, working |
| **Pending** | Awaiting action |
| **Processing** | Being worked on |
| **Completed/Fulfilled** | Done |
| **Cancelled** | Stopped |
| **Declined/Rejected** | Not approved |
| **Draft** | Saved but not published |
| **Expired** | Past validity date |
| **Shortlisted** | Application added to candidate shortlist |
| **Interview** | Interview stage of the application pipeline |
| **Offer** | Job offer has been sent to the candidate |
| **Hired** | Candidate has been hired |
| **Open** | Replacement request posted, awaiting a match |
| **Matched** | At least one candidate has been offered the replacement shift |
| **Confirmed** | A candidate has accepted and the replacement is confirmed |
| **Proposed** | Candidate has been suggested for a replacement (not yet formally offered) |
| **Offered** | Foundation has formally offered the replacement shift to a candidate |
