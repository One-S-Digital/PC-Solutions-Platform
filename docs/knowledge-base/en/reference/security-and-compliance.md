## GDPR Compliance

### What is GDPR?

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is a European privacy law that protects personal data of individuals in the EU and Switzerland.

### Your GDPR Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

| Right | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Access** | Request a copy of your data |
| **Rectification** | Correct inaccurate data |
| **Erasure** | Request deletion of your data |
| **Portability** | Receive data in a portable format |
| **Restriction** | Limit how we process your data |
| **Objection** | Object to certain processing |

### Exercising Your Rights

**To request data access or export:**
1. Go to Settings → Privacy & Data
2. Click "Request Data Export"
3. Receive download link via email

**To request data deletion:**
1. Go to Settings → Privacy & Data
2. Click "Request Data Deletion"
3. Confirm your request
4. Data is deleted per GDPR requirements

**For other requests:**
1. Create a support ticket
2. Select "Privacy" as category
3. Describe your request
4. We'll respond within 30 days

## Data Encryption

### Data in Transit

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using:

- **TLS 1.2+** - Transport Layer Security
- **HTTPS** - Secure connection protocol

This means your data is protected from interception while traveling over the internet.

### Data at Rest

Data stored in our systems is encrypted:

- **Database encryption** - Stored data is encrypted
- **Backup encryption** - Backups are encrypted
- **File encryption** - Uploaded files are protected

### Password Security

- Passwords are **never stored in plain text**
- We use **secure hashing algorithms**
- Passwords are **salted** for additional security

## Account Security Tips

### Strong Passwords

Create a strong password:
- ✅ At least 8 characters
- ✅ Mix of letters, numbers, symbols
- ✅ Unique to this platform
- ❌ Don't use personal information
- ❌ Don't use common words

### Protect Your Account

| Do | Don't |
|----|----|
| ✅ Use a unique password | ❌ Share your password |
| ✅ Log out on shared devices | ❌ Use public computers for sensitive tasks |
| ✅ Keep your email secure | ❌ Ignore suspicious activity |
| ✅ Update your password regularly | ❌ Use the same password everywhere |

### Recognizing Suspicious Activity

Watch for:
- Unexpected password reset emails
- Login notifications from unknown locations
- Changes you didn't make to your account

**If you notice suspicious activity:**
1. Change your password immediately
2. Check your email account security
3. Contact support

### Phishing Awareness

We will **never** ask you to:
- Send your password via email
- Click suspicious links
- Provide payment information via email

**Legitimate communications:**
- Come from official domains
- Don't ask for your password
- Can be verified by logging into the platform directly
