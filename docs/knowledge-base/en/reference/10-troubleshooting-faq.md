## Account Issues

### Can't Sign In

**Problem:** Unable to log into your account.

**Solutions:**

1. **Check your email address**
   - Make sure you're using the correct email
   - Check for typos

2. **Reset your password**
   - Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
   - Follow the reset instructions

3. **Check Caps Lock**
   - Passwords are case-sensitive
   - Make sure Caps Lock is off

4. **Clear browser cache**
   - Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
   - Clear cookies and cache
   - Try again

5. **Try a different browser**
   - Test with Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
   - Disable browser extensions

**Still stuck?** [Contact support](#contact-support)

---

### Email Verification Not Received

**Problem:** You didn't receive the email verification code.

**Solutions:**

1. **Check spam/junk folder**
   - The email may have been filtered
   - Mark as "Not Spam" if found

2. **Wait a few minutes**
   - Email delivery can take 1-5 minutes
   - Check again shortly

3. **Resend the code**
   - Click "Resend code" on the verification screen
   - Wait 30 seconds between attempts

4. **Check the correct inbox**
   - Make sure you're checking the email you registered with
   - Not a different email address

5. **Add to contacts**
   - Add no-reply@procreche.ch to your contacts
   - This prevents future filtering

6. **Try a different email**
   - If using a work email, try a personal email
   - Some corporate filters block verification emails

---

### Password Reset Issues

**Problem:** Password reset link not working or not received.

**Solutions:**

1. **Check spam folder**
   - Reset emails often get filtered

2. **Request a new link**
   - Reset links expire after a short time
   - Request a fresh one

3. **Check the correct email**
   - The link is sent to your registered email

4. **Complete reset quickly**
   - Links expire, so act promptly
   - Don't close the browser tab

---

### Account Locked

**Problem:** Your account appears to be locked.

**Causes:**
- Too many failed login attempts
- Security concern detected
- Administrative action

**Solutions:**

1. **Wait and try again**
   - Lockouts often expire after 15-30 minutes

2. **Reset your password**
   - This may unlock your account

3. **Contact support**
   - If lockout persists, reach out for help

---

### Profile Not Updating

**Problem:** Changes to your profile aren't saving.

**Solutions:**

1. **Click Save**
   - Make sure you click the "Save" button
   - Don't just close the page

2. **Check required fields**
   - Look for red error messages
   - Fill in all required fields

3. **Check file sizes**
   - Images must be under size limits
   - Try a smaller file

4. **Refresh and retry**
   - Reload the page
   - Make changes again

5. **Check internet connection**
   - Ensure you're connected
   - Try again

## Subscription & Billing Issues

### Payment Failed

**Problem:** Your subscription payment didn't go through.

**Solutions:**

1. **Check payment details**
   - Verify card number, expiry, CVV
   - Ensure sufficient funds

2. **Try a different card**
   - Use an alternative payment method

3. **Contact your bank**
   - They may be blocking the transaction

4. **Contact support**
   - We can help resolve payment issues

---

### Wrong Plan Features

**Problem:** You can't access features you think you should have.

**Solutions:**

1. **Check your current plan**
   - Go to Settings → Billing
   - Verify your subscription status

2. **Verify feature availability**
   - Check [Billing Guide](../guides/9-billing-and-subscriptions.md) for feature matrix
   - Some features require higher plans

3. **Check subscription status**
   - Make sure subscription is "Active"
   - Renew if expired

4. **Contact support if needed**
   - Report if features are incorrectly restricted

---

### Cancellation Problems

**Problem:** Issues with cancelling your subscription.

**Solutions:**

1. **Submit cancellation request**
   - Go to Settings → Billing
   - Click "Cancel Subscription"
   - Complete the process

2. **Check status**
   - Cancellation may take time to process
   - You retain access until period ends

3. **Contact support**
   - If you can't cancel, reach out for help

### Invoice Questions

## Feature-Specific Issues

### Leads Not Appearing

**Problem:** Parent leads aren't showing up (Foundations).

**Solutions:**

1. **Check subscription**
   - Leads require Essential+ plan
   - Verify your subscription

2. **Check filters**
   - Clear any active filters
   - Try "All" status

3. **Refresh the page**
   - New leads may not show immediately
   - Try refreshing

4. **Check lead limits**
   - Essential plan has limited leads
   - You may have reached your limit

---

### Orders Not Showing

**Problem:** Orders aren't appearing in your dashboard.

**Solutions:**

1. **Check the correct section**
   - Orders are in the Orders section
   - Not on the main dashboard

2. **Check filters**
   - Clear date and status filters

3. **Refresh**
   - New orders may take a moment to appear

4. **Contact support**
   - If orders are missing, report it

---

### Messages Not Sending

**Problem:** Can't send messages through the platform.

**Solutions:**

1. **Check internet connection**
   - Ensure you're online

2. **Check message length**
   - Very long messages may fail
   - Try a shorter message

3. **Check attachments**
   - Large attachments may fail
   - Try without attachments

4. **Refresh and retry**
   - Reload the messaging page
   - Try sending again

---

### Files Not Uploading

**Problem:** Unable to upload files.

**Solutions:**

1. **Check file size**
   - Files must be under size limits:
     - Images: 5-10 MB
     - Documents: 25 MB

2. **Check file type**
   - Supported: PDF, DOC, DOCX, JPG, PNG
   - Try a different format

3. **Try a different browser**
   - Some browsers handle uploads differently

4. **Check internet speed**
   - Large files need stable connection

---

### Analytics Not Loading

**Problem:** Analytics dashboard isn't displaying data.

**Solutions:**

1. **Check subscription**
   - Analytics requires Professional plan

2. **Check date range**
   - Select a period with data
   - Try "Last 30 Days"

3. **Wait for data**
   - New accounts may not have data yet

4. **Refresh the page**
   - Analytics can take time to load

## Technical Issues

### Page Not Loading

**Problem:** Pages won't load or show errors.

**Solutions:**

1. **Refresh the page**
   - Press F5 or Ctrl+R (Cmd+R on Mac)

2. **Clear browser cache**
   - Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows)
   - Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
   - Clear cache and cookies

3. **Check internet connection**
   - Test other websites
   - Restart your router if needed

4. **Try a different browser**
   - Use Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge

5. **Disable extensions**
   - Ad blockers can interfere
   - Try in incognito/private mode

6. **Check system status**
   - The platform may be under maintenance

---

### Error Messages

**Problem:** You're seeing error messages.

**Common errors and solutions:**

| Error | Meaning | Solution |
|-------|---------|----------|
| "Session expired" | You were logged out | Sign in again |
| "Network error" | Connection issue | Check internet, retry |
| "Server error" | Platform issue | Wait and retry |
| "Permission denied" | No access to feature | Check subscription |
| "Invalid input" | Form error | Check the highlighted field |

## Data & Privacy

### Data Export/Download

**How to export your data:**

1. Go to Settings → Privacy & Data
2. Click "Request Data Export"
3. Wait for email with download link
4. Download within 48 hours

---

### Account Deletion Request (GDPR)

**How to request account deletion:**

1. Go to Settings → Privacy & Data
2. Click "Request Data Deletion"
3. Confirm your request
4. Your request is processed per GDPR requirements

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

---

### Privacy Settings

**How to adjust privacy settings:**

1. Go to Settings → Privacy & Data
2. Toggle visibility settings
3. Changes save automatically

---

### Data Retention

**How long is data kept?**

- Active accounts: Data retained while active
- Deleted accounts: Data removed per GDPR (typically 30 days)
- Logs: Retained for security purposes (limited time)

## Contact Support

### How to Contact Support

If you can't resolve your issue:

1. **Create a support ticket:**
   - Go to Support in the sidebar
   - Click "Create Ticket"
   - Fill in details
   - Submit

2. **Include helpful information:**
   - What you were trying to do
   - What happened instead
   - Error messages (if any)
   - Screenshots (helpful!)
   - Browser and device info

### Support Ticket Categories

| Category | Use for |
|----------|---------|
| **General** | General questions |
| **Technical** | Bugs, errors, features not working |
| **Billing** | Payment, invoices, subscriptions |
| **Feature Request** | Suggestions for improvements |

### Support Ticket Priorities

| Priority | Use when |
|----------|----------|
| **Low** | Minor issue, not urgent |
| **Medium** | Normal importance |
| **High** | Significant impact on work |
| **Urgent** | Critical, cannot use platform |

### Expected Response Times

- **Urgent:** Within hours (business hours)
- **High:** Within 1 business day
- **Medium:** Within 2 business days
- **Low:** Within 3-5 business days

### Support Hours

Monday - Friday
9:00 AM - 6:00 PM CET

*Note: Urgent issues may receive attention outside these hours*
