# Role-Based Dashboards & Settings Guide

This document outlines the structure and content of the role-based dashboards and the shared settings pages.

## 1. Role-Based Dashboards

Each user role has a dedicated dashboard that serves as their main landing page after login. The dashboard should provide a summary of relevant information and quick access to key actions.

- **Foundation Dashboard:**
  - **Stats:** Number of new parent enquiries, open job applications, upcoming appointments.
  - **Quick Links:** "Post a New Job", "Browse Marketplace", "View Parent Leads".
  - **Recent Activity:** A feed of recent events (e.g., "New application from John Doe", "Parent enquiry from Jane Smith").

- **Supplier Dashboard:**
  - **Stats:** Number of new orders, total sales volume, top-selling products.
  - **Quick Links:** "Add a New Product", "View Orders", "Update Company Profile".
  - **Recent Activity:** A feed of recent orders.

- **Service Provider Dashboard:**
  - **Stats:** Number of new service requests, upcoming appointments.
  - **Quick Links:** "Add a New Service", "View Requests", "Update Company Profile".
  - **Recent Activity:** A feed of recent service requests.

- **Educator/Candidate Dashboard:**
  - **Stats:** Number of applications submitted, application statuses (viewed, accepted, rejected).
  - **Quick Links:** "Search Job Board", "Update My Profile", "View My Applications".
  - **Recent Activity:** A feed of updates on their job applications.

- **Parent Dashboard:**
  - **Stats:** Number of enquiries sent, number of responses received.
  - **Quick Links:** "Find a Daycare", "View My Enquiries", "Update My Profile".
  - **Recent Activity:** A feed of responses from foundations.

## 2. Settings Pages

The settings area is accessible to all logged-in users and is organized into tabs. The available tabs and fields depend on the user's role.

- **Company Details:**
  - For organization roles (Foundation, Supplier, Service Provider).
  - Allows editing of all organization profile fields (name, logo, about text, etc.).

- **Contact Preferences:**
  - Allows users to manage their primary contact email and phone number.

- **Notification Toggles:**
  - A series of toggles to enable or disable different types of email notifications (e.g., "Notify me of new messages", "Send me a weekly digest").

- **Team Members (for organizations):**
  - Allows organization admins to invite, remove, and manage the roles of team members.

- **GDPR & Data Privacy:**
  - Provides options for users to download their data or request account deletion, in compliance with GDPR.
