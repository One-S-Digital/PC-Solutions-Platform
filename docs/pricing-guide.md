# ProCrèche Solutions - Pricing Guide

## Overview

ProCrèche Solutions offers flexible pricing plans for different types of organizations and users. All plans include multilingual support (English, French, German) and are billed in Swiss Francs (CHF).

## Pricing Structure

### Annual Billing Benefits
- **Save up to 10%** on all plans when billed annually
- Annual equivalent monthly rate shown when annual billing is selected

## How to Change Plans

1. **Navigate to Settings** → Billing & Subscription (Foundation users only)
2. **Select your desired plan** from the available options
3. **Complete payment** through our secure Stripe integration
4. **Changes take effect** immediately upon successful payment

## Payment Methods

- **Credit/Debit Cards:** Visa, MasterCard, American Express
- **Bank Transfer:** Available for annual billing (contact support)
- **Stripe Security:** All payments processed securely through Stripe

## Billing Cycle Information

- **Monthly Billing:** Charged monthly on the same date
- **Annual Billing:** Charged once per year with 10% savings
- **24/7 Invoice Access:** Available through Stripe customer portal
- **Currency:** All billing in Swiss Francs (CHF)
